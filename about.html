About Helping Hands Food Bank

Helping Hands Food Bank is a non-profit organization dedicated to fighting hunger in the Georgian community. We work with local businesses, farmers, and individuals to collect and distribute food to those in need.

We believe that everyone deserves access to healthy and nutritious food. Through our food bank programs, we provide emergency food assistance to families, individuals, and seniors facing food insecurity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to alleviate hunger in our community by providing access to nutritious food for those in need.

Our Vision

We envision a community where everyone has access to the food they need to live a healthy and productive life.

Find Help

If you are facing food insecurity, Helping Hands Food Bank is here to help. We offer a variety of programs to assist you, including:

  • Emergency Food Assistance: We provide emergency food boxes to families and individuals in need.
  • Senior Meal Program: We deliver hot meals to homebound seniors.
  • Children's Feeding Program: We provide healthy snacks and meals to children at risk of hunger.

To find out more about our programs and how to qualify, please visit our Programs and Services page or contact us directly.

Get Involved

There are many ways you can help fight hunger in your community. Here are a few ways you can get involved with Helping Hands Food Bank:

  • Donate Food: We accept donations of non-perishable food items.
  • Volunteer your Time: We have a variety of volunteer opportunities available.
  • Host a Food Drive: Organize a food drive at your workplace, school, or place of worship.
  • Fundraise for Helping Hands: Help us raise money to continue our fight against hunger.

To learn more about how you can get involved, please visit our Get Involved page.
